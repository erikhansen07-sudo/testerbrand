Brand Foundation

What is Gridstrike?

An interactive LED tile arena where physical movement meets real-time technology. Players compete on a live grid of hundreds of color-reactive floor tiles — green to strike, red to avoid, blue to regroup. No gear. No experience. Just show up and move.

⚡

The Experience

Step onto the Grid. Every tile beneath you is alive — responding in real time to where you stand and how fast you move. Green lights your target. Red means danger. Blue means breathe. Every round the rules change and the grid escalates.

👥

Built for All Players

Ages 4 to 94. Solo warriors, birthday squads, competitive crews, corporate teams. No skill required. The floor is the equalizer — everyone starts from zero every round.

🟢

The Strike Mechanic

Hit the green tiles to score. Dodge the red. Use the blue to regroup. Simple to learn in 10 seconds. Impossible to master in a lifetime. The grid always fights back harder.

🔥

Events & Bookings

Birthday takeovers. Corporate showdowns. Date nights with actual stakes. Gridstrike runs itself — staff, scoring, sound, and trash talk fully included.

Brand Voice

Tone & Personality

The voice matches the theme. Same DNA, different energy level.

We Are

Decisive — Every word earns its place. No padding, no filler.

Confident — We don't ask if you're ready. We assume you are.

Inclusive — Intense, not intimidating. The grid accepts everyone.

Action-first — Verbs over adjectives. What you do, not what we have.

Playful — The score is serious. The vibe is not.

We Are Not

Corporate — No synergy. No leverage. No end-to-end solutions.

Over-hyped — One exclamation point max per campaign. Ever.

Over-explained — Don't describe the tech. Describe how it feels.

Childish — Family-friendly doesn't mean dumbed down.

Passive — The floor is live. The copy should be too.

Game Mechanics

The Tile Language

No tutorial needed. The grid teaches itself in the first 10 seconds. Three colors. Three meanings. One rule: survive the round.

Green — Strike It

Active target tile. Hit it to score. Miss it, lose your streak. The whole game lives here.

Red — Danger Zone

Step here and lose points. The floor is hunting you. Stay fast or get punished.

Blue — Safe Zone

Regroup. Catch your breath. It won't last long — the grid always takes it back.

Game Modes

Strike Sessions

Two 18-minute rounds per session. Multiple game modes keep the grid unpredictable — even regulars never play the same game twice.

⚡

GRIDSTRIKE
