Brand Foundation

What is Gridstrike?

An interactive LED tile arena where physical movement meets real-time tech. Players step onto a live grid of hundreds of pressure-sensitive, color-reactive floor tiles and compete in fast-paced team games designed for all ages and skill levels.

⚡

The Experience

Step onto the Grid. Every tile beneath you pulses with light, responding in real time to where you stand, what you do, and how fast you move. Danger is red. Safety is green. Score is electric blue. And every round, the rules change.

👥

Built for All Players

Ages 4 to 94. Solo warriors, birthday squads, competitive crews, corporate teams. No prior skill. No equipment. Just show up, step on the grid, and compete. The floor does the rest.

🎮

The Strike Mechanic

Unlike passive entertainment, every session has an objective — eliminate the grid before it eliminates you. Strike the lit tiles, clear the zones, outlast your opponents. The grid always fights back.

🔥

Events & Parties

Birthday takeovers. Team-building blitzes. Corporate showdowns. Date nights with stakes. Gridstrike is the arena that runs itself — staff, scoring, sound, and smack-talk included.

Brand Voice

Tone & Personality

Gridstrike is hype without cringe. Competitive without exclusion. Technical without complexity. The voice is the MC in the room who makes every player feel like a champion.

We Are

Electric — Every word should feel like the moment before a round starts

Inclusive — Nobody gets left out. The grid accepts everyone.

Punchy — Short sentences. Active verbs. No filler.

Confident — We don't ask if you're ready. We tell you to bring it.

Playful — We don't take ourselves too seriously. But the score? That's real.

We Are Not

Corporate — No "synergy." No "leveraging." No "end-to-end solutions."

Aggressive — Intense, yes. Mean, no. The competition is on the grid, not in the copy.

Childish — Family-friendly doesn't mean dumbed down.

Over-explained — Don't describe the tech. Describe how it feels.

Passive — The floor is live. The copy should be too.

Game Mechanics

The Tile Language

Players don't need instructions. The grid teaches itself. Color is the only language that matters — and it's universal.

Blue — Strike It

Active tile. Hit it to score. Miss it and lose your streak.

Red — Danger Zone

Step here and lose points. The floor is hunting you.

Green — Safe Zone

Regroup. Catch your breath. It won't last long.

Gold — Power Strike

Rare tile. Maximum points. First to reach it wins the round.

Game Modes

Strike Sessions

Every session runs two 18-minute rounds. Multiple game modes keep the grid unpredictable — even regulars never play the same game twice.

⚡

GRIDSTRIKE

Core Mode

The original. Strike every blue tile before time runs out. The grid speeds up. You don't.

🔥

BLACKOUT

Elimination

The entire grid is live. One wrong tile and you're out. Last player standing takes the grid.

👥

SQUAD STRIKE

Team Mode

Divide the grid. Control your zone. The team that lights the most tiles in 18 minutes wins the arena.

🏆

POWER GRID

Challenge Mode

Gold tiles only. Appear and vanish in 2 seconds. Hit them all or watch your score collapse.

🌊

WAVE MODE

Survival

Strike tiles sweep across the grid in waves. Keep up or fall behind. The waves never stop.

🎉

PARTY STRIKE

All Ages

Slower pace. Bigger rewards. Maximum chaos. Designed for mixed groups and first-time players.
