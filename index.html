Brand Foundation

What is Gridstrike?

An interactive LED tile arena where physical movement meets real-time technology. Players compete on a live grid of hundreds of pressure-sensitive, color-reactive floor tiles — no gear, no experience, just show up and strike.

⚡

The Experience

Step onto the Grid. Every tile beneath you is alive — responding in real time to where you stand and how fast you move. Danger is red. Score is lit. Every round the rules change and the grid escalates.

👥

Built for All Players

Ages 4 to 94. Solo warriors, birthday squads, competitive crews, corporate teams. No skill required. The floor is the equalizer — everyone starts from zero every round.

🎯

The Strike Mechanic

Every session has one objective: light the grid before it lights you up. Strike the active tiles, clear the zones, outlast your opponents. The grid always fights back harder.

🔥

Events & Bookings

Birthday takeovers. Corporate showdowns. Date nights with actual stakes. Gridstrike runs itself — staff, scoring, sound, and trash talk fully included.

Brand Voice

Tone & Personality

The voice matches the theme. Same DNA, different energy level.

We Are

Decisive — Every word earns its place. No padding, no filler.

Confident — We don't ask if you're ready. We assume you are.

Inclusive — Intense, not intimidating. The grid accepts everyone.

Action-first — Verbs over adjectives. What you do, not what we have.

Playful — The score is serious. The vibe is not.

We Are Not

Corporate — No synergy. No leverage. No end-to-end solutions.

Over-hyped — One exclamation point max per campaign. Ever.

Over-explained — Don't describe the tech. Describe how it feels.

Childish — Family-friendly doesn't mean dumbed down.

Passive — The floor is live. The copy should be too.

Game Mechanics

The Tile Language

No tutorial needed. The grid teaches itself in the first 10 seconds. Color is the only language that matters — and it's universal.

Blue — Strike It

Active tile. Hit it to score. Miss it, lose your streak.

Red — Danger Zone

Step here and lose points. The floor is hunting you.

Green — Safe Zone

Regroup. It won't last long.

Gold — Power Strike

Rare tile. Maximum points. First to reach it wins the round.

Game Modes

Strike Sessions

Two 18-minute rounds per session. Multiple game modes keep the grid unpredictable — even regulars never play the same game twice.

⚡

GRIDSTRIKE

Core Mode

Strike every blue tile before time runs out. The grid speeds up. You don't.

🔥

BLACKOUT

Elimination

One wrong tile and you're out. Last player standing takes the grid.

👥

SQUAD STRIKE

Team Mode

Divide the grid. Control your zone. Most tiles lit at the buzzer wins.

🏆

POWER GRID

Challenge

Gold tiles only. Appear and vanish in 2 seconds. Hit them all or collapse.

🌊

WAVE MODE

Survival

Strike waves sweep across the floor. Keep up or fall behind. Waves never stop.

🎉

PARTY STRIKE

All Ages

Slower pace. Bigger rewards. Maximum chaos. Built for mixed groups.
